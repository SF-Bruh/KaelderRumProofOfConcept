About Us

Reimagining Storage
for Modern Denmark

Across Denmark, thousands of basements sit empty while storage costs continue to rise. KælderRum was built to bridge that gap — creating a trusted marketplace where homeowners monetize unused kælder (basement) space, and renters find affordable, convenient storage right in their own neighborhoods.

We believe that the best storage solution is one that already exists. By unlocking the potential of residential basements throughout Copenhagen, Aarhus, Odense, and beyond, we are building a more sustainable, community-driven alternative to traditional self-storage facilities.

Every space on our platform is verified, insured, and backed by our trust guarantee. Whether you need to store seasonal gear, business inventory, or furniture between moves, KælderRum makes it effortless.
